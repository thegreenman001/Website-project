A little about me


Hi! I am a marketing and communications specialist currently based in the Stockholm region, Sweden. Originally, I am from Bucharest, Romania but moved to Sweden in February 2020. I love indulging in good content, regardless of the format, I am an avid consumer of everything, you name it, books, podcasts, films, series, videos and so on. I love distance running and competing in race events.

I am fluent and have professional experience with the following languages:

  • English
  • Spanish
  • Swedish
  • Romanian

I have been constantly forced by the circumstances to adapt to fast-paced environments, gaining value and insight from many different industries. Starting from managing Social Media accounts for Adevărul, one of Romania’s most important daily publications, writing for bursa.ro to supporting Talktalk’s sales operations, Suunto’s amazing sports watches, apps, and e-commerce, I am nowadays mostly working with marketing, having managed a broad range of digital marketing campaigns, with an emphasis on marketing automation. I was one of Mellifiq’s main marketing administrators and Academic Work’s main email marketing specialist, with a significant contribution to the marketing automation strategy for Young Professionals Attraction Index 2022.

If you want a more detailed overview of my work experience don’t hesitate to take a look at my CV (in English). Nevertheless, I have created a wide range of content throughout my career, which I have detailed in the portfolio section of this website.
