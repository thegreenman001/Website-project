A little about me


Hi there! I'm a results-driven marketing and communications specialist based in the Stockholm region, Sweden, with a strong dedication to helping businesses grow and refine their digital persona. My rich experience with different business models can help translating your business requirements into functioning digital solutions. Whether it’s your website, CRM, or marketing automation platform, I excel at delivering solutions that fulfil exactly those business needs that are critical to creating and converting leads, delivering the message to the right audience at the right time or facilitate communication between your business and its customers.

Besides, as an avid consumer of various forms of content, I understand the power of captivating storytelling and engaging communication. I believe that compelling and engaging content is one of the core assets of successful digital marketing strategies. I can craft social media campaigns, blog posts, or email marketing strategies that resonate with the group they’re intended to reach.

With fluency in multiple languages, including English, Spanish, Swedish, and Romanian, I possess the ability to connect with diverse and unique target audiences and navigate global markets. I thrive in both fast-paced as well as risk-averse and heavily regulated environments and have a proven track record of adapting quickly to industry trends and technologies.
